# 双路线技术策略

5TD uses a dual-route strategy: a near-term RGB-only engineering route and an enhanced research route.

## RGB-only Engineering Route

The RGB route centers on [[HBD-Net-RT Baseline]]. It is the current runnable system for perception, mapping, planning, safety, and control-command formatting.

## Enhanced Research Route

The research route includes [[RGB Vision Research]], [[Transformer Fusion Research]], [[RL Navigation Research]], [[Diffusion Planner Research]], and [[VLM Supervisor Research]]. These tracks should improve perception, risk modeling, trajectory proposals, annotation support, or supervisory reasoning.

## Non-Negotiable Interface

Enhanced modules must not directly command motors. Their outputs must be converted into risk maps, candidate trajectories, waypoints, or supervisory signals and then checked by [[DWAPlanner]], [[SafetyStateMachine]], and [[Safety Filter]].
