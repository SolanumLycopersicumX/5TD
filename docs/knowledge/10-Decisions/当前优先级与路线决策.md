# 当前优先级与路线决策

The current priority is to keep the RGB-only HBD-Net-RT baseline runnable, evaluate it under actual tunnel conditions, and add sensor fusion only where RGB-only safety evidence is insufficient.

## Current Order

1. Keep [[HBD-Net-RT Baseline]] runnable.
2. Evaluate RGB-only detection of free space, obstacles, workers, engineering vehicles, and [[Hard Boundary]].
3. Use right-side distance sensing, LiDAR, or fusion if trench-margin safety cannot be validated with RGB alone.
4. Keep RL, diffusion, VLM, and Transformer work behind [[Costmap 与 Risk Grid]], [[DWAPlanner]], and [[Safety Filter]].

## Why This Matters

The repo is not fusion-only and not legacy-only. The practical MVP is RGB-only, while the enhanced research route remains available for safety or performance upgrades.
