# 安全约束导航

Safety-constrained navigation means perception and planning are never enough by themselves. The final command is constrained by risk, boundary, confidence, and path-feasibility checks.

## Layers

1. [[Perception 模块]] estimates obstacles, passable area, hard boundaries, edges, and risk.
2. [[Mapping 模块]] turns hard boundaries and non-passable space into occupancy and risk.
3. [[DWAPlanner]] rejects trajectories that collide with high-risk areas.
4. [[SafetyStateMachine]] escalates from normal operation to cautious, slowdown, stop, or manual takeover.
5. [[ControlCommand]] forces zero speed and braking in STOP or TAKEOVER states.
