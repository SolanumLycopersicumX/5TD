# 验收指标

Acceptance should combine navigation, safety, model, and latency metrics.

## Metric Areas

- Navigation behavior in representative tunnel scenarios.
- Safety behavior around [[Hard Boundary]], [[Trench Keep-out]], workers, and no-path situations.
- Model quality for perception outputs.
- Latency against the baseline budget in [[延迟基准]].

## Current Training Targets

The HBD-Net-RT training guide records target perception metrics: detection mAP@0.5 at least 75%, worker recall at least 90%, passable mIoU at least 88%, and ditch boundary IoU at least 70%.
