# Research Tracks

Research modules are kept separate from runnable baselines until their interfaces are stable.

- `rgb_vision/`: pure-RGB perception improvements beyond HBD-Net-RT.
- `transformer_fusion/`: LiDAR-RGB BEV fusion, cross-attention, and temporal context.
- `rl_navigation/`: safety-constrained RL policies and reward experiments.
- `diffusion_planner/`: candidate trajectory generation.
- `vlm_supervisor/`: open-vocabulary annotation support and low-frequency supervision.
